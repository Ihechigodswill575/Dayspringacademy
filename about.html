Our Story

About Day Spring Academy

Rooted in a tradition of excellence, we have been shaping minds and futures since 2010. Discover who we are, what we stand for, and the people who make it all possible.

Purpose & Direction

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To Educate, Inspire, and Empower

Day Spring Academy is committed to providing a world-class education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in a rapidly evolving world. We foster critical thinking, creativity, and compassion in every learner.

Our Vision

Leaders Who Change the World

We envision a generation of confident, morally grounded, and intellectually exceptional graduates who lead with integrity and drive positive change in their communities and beyond. Day Spring Academy aspires to be the most transformative academic institution in the region.

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Over 16 Years

2010

Foundation

Day Spring Academy was founded with 80 students and 12 dedicated teachers, opening its doors with a mission to serve the community with quality education.

2015

First High School Class

We launched our high school department and our inaugural graduating class achieved a 100% university placement rate — a record we've maintained ever since.

2020

Digital Learning Expansion

We invested in state-of-the-art ICT infrastructure, launched our digital library, and adopted a blended learning model to future-proof our education delivery.

2026

Today & Beyond

With 500+ students, 50+ expert faculty, and a new campus expansion underway, Day Spring Academy continues to set the standard for academic excellence in the region.
