Our Programs

Academic Programs

Rigorous, engaging, and future-focused curricula for every stage of your child's educational journey — from foundational learning to university preparation.

📚
Elementary · Grades 1–3

Early Foundations Program

A warm, nurturing environment that builds literacy, numeracy, social, and emotional intelligence through play-based, inquiry-led learning.

English Language Mathematics Basic Science Creative Arts
Grades 1–3 Ages 6–9

Our Early Foundations Program prioritises joyful discovery. Through structured play, storytelling, and hands-on projects, young learners develop a love for school and the confidence to ask questions. Our classroom ratio never exceeds 1:15.

🌿
Elementary · Grades 4–6

Upper Elementary Program

Deepening academic rigour while fostering independence, project-based learning, and collaborative problem-solving skills.

English Maths General Science Social Studies ICT Basics
Grades 4–6 Ages 9–12

Students in this phase engage with more complex texts, explore scientific inquiry through experiments, and develop their first formal writing skills. We introduce structured homework and research-based projects to build good academic habits.

🔭
Middle School · Grades 7–9

Middle School Core Program

A challenging and exciting transitional program that bridges the gap between childhood curiosity and adolescent academic ambition.

Literature Algebra Biology Chemistry ICT Civic Education
Grades 7–9 Ages 12–15

At this stage, students take on more responsibility for their own learning. Subject specialists teach each discipline, debate clubs and science clubs are available, and students begin to explore career pathways and personal interests.

🎨
Middle School · Enrichment

Arts & Technology Enrichment

Optional enrichment program combining STEM projects, visual arts, and digital media for creatively inclined middle schoolers.

Robotics Visual Arts Digital Media Music
Grades 7–9 Optional

Held twice weekly after school, this program gives students access to our robotics lab, recording studio, and art studio. Projects are showcased at our annual Creativity Fair each December.

🏛️
High School · Sciences

Science Stream

Intensive preparation for STEM university programs — medicine, engineering, computer science, and the natural sciences.

Physics Chemistry Biology Further Mathematics English
Grades 10–12 Ages 15–18

Our Science Stream has produced some of the country's top medical students and engineers. Students benefit from fully equipped labs, university-level reading materials, and mentorship from industry professionals.

📊
High School · Commerce

Commerce & Social Sciences Stream

Building business acumen, economic literacy, and social insight for careers in law, finance, business, and public administration.

Economics Accounting Commerce Government English
Grades 10–12 Ages 15–18

Students in this stream participate in a school-run enterprise where they manage a real budget, keep accounts, and present financial reports — giving them practical experience that sets them apart at university and beyond.

Beyond the Classroom

Extracurricular Activities

A rich co-curricular life ensures every student discovers their passion and develops as a whole person.

⚽

Sports

Football, basketball, athletics, swimming, and more — our sports program builds teamwork, discipline, and resilience.

🎭

Arts & Drama

Annual school plays, visual arts exhibitions, and a thriving music department for creatively gifted students.

🧠

Debate Club

Our award-winning debate team competes nationally, sharpening public speaking and critical thinking skills.

💻

Coding & Robotics

Weekly coding sessions and an active robotics team that regularly competes in regional and national competitions.
