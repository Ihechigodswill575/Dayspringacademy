Requirements

What You Need

📋 Entry Requirements

  • Most recent school report card
  • Birth certificate or valid ID
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Proof of address
  • Entry assessment (on-site)

📅 Admissions Timeline

  • Applications open: January
  • Assessments: February–March
  • Acceptance letters: April
  • Enrolment deadline: May 30
  • New term begins: September

💰 Fees & Financial Aid

  • Registration fee: Non-refundable
  • Tuition payable per term
  • Merit scholarships available (Top 5%)
  • Need-based bursaries considered
  • Contact us for full fee schedule

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-to-teacher ratio?

Our average ratio is 1:18 for elementary and 1:22 for secondary. Classes are kept small so every student receives personal attention.

Is there a waiting list?

Yes — popular grades fill up quickly. We recommend applying early. A waiting list is maintained for late applicants.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Yes, subject to space availability. The same assessment and documentation process applies.

Are there scholarship opportunities?

We offer merit-based scholarships to the top 5% of applicants and limited need-based bursaries. Mention your interest in the form above.
